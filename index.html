About FreshGuard

FreshGuard Technologies

FreshGuard Technologies is a software engineering team focused on making cold-chain logistics easier to supervise, document and improve. Our product, ColdTrack, connects operational teams with shipment, sensor and alert data so quality decisions can be made with evidence instead of guesswork.

Mission

Help food distribution teams protect product quality by making cold-chain conditions visible, measurable and actionable.

Vision

Become a trusted technology partner for safer, more efficient and more traceable food logistics in Peru and Latin America.

What we build

We design web tools for shipment registration, sensor assignment, telemetry tracking, incident alerts and delivery status control.

Who we support

ColdTrack is oriented to distributors, logistics coordinators and quality teams that need a clear view of every refrigerated delivery.

Why it matters

Better visibility reduces product loss, improves response times and creates traceable records for continuous improvement.
